CLS journey — raw 4-D (colour = dim 3) i

✕ The CLS token's journey — all 4 dims, no projection. One shape = one test image's CLS vector (sphere 0 · cube 1 · pyramid 2): x,y,z = dims 0–2, colour = dim 3. Every image starts at the same point, then CLSout = CLSin + bo + Σh ah(image)·dirh each head h pushes along its fixed arrow dirh by an image-dependent scalar ah — one new axis per head. The final LayerNorm stretches the separating axes (γ≈2.6) into three clusters. The glowing octahedron is your drawing; the white trail is its full 7-stage path.
